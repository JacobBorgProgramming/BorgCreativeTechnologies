What is Coconut?

Coconut is a Safari browser extension for blocking cookies and unwanted content on harmful websites.

Designed for MacOS.

Built for Apple's Safari browser using Cocoa and JSON.

An independent database.

We have built our own database to provide a better, dynamic extension.

Built for privacy.

Coconut will block cookies on websites that are known not to respect your privacy.

Please read the terms and conditions before use. By downloading Coconut, you automatically agree to the terms, conditions, and privacy policy.
