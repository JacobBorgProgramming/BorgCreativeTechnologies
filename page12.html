Advertising Guidelines

As a family-friendly organization, we prohibit any ads that are not rated for general audiences. This includes the ad's content, product, and link. We do not allow offensive ads, ads that misinform, ads for products or services that are not family-friendly, ads that promote illegal behavior, or ads promoting illegal product. Links to your website are NOT to be shortened links. This is to insure security, privacy, and quality. (We have a limited number of ad slots available.
